// Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

I have a video idea but don't know how to start. Can you help? +

Absolutely! Just reach out via email or Instagram and we'll hop on a quick discovery call. I'll help you shape the idea, plan the structure, and figure out the best edit style for your audience.

How can editing actually make my video more engaging? +

Great editing controls the pace of your story — it keeps viewers hooked with the right music, cuts, effects, and pacing. I use hook-first structures, retention curves, and platform-specific techniques to maximize watch time.

Do you offer motion graphics and special effects? +

Yes! Animated titles, lower-thirds, kinetic text, custom transitions, and After Effects motion graphics are all part of my toolkit. Let me know your vision and I'll bring it to life.

Can you edit for both YouTube and Instagram at the same time? +

Definitely. I optimize each video for its platform — aspect ratios, pacing, captions, and hook length all differ between YouTube long-form and Instagram Reels. You'll get platform-native exports for each.

How much creative control do I have? +

Complete control. I work closely with you to understand your style and preferences before starting. Every delivery includes revisions until you're 100% happy with the result.

What's your typical turnaround time? +

Short-form Reels and Shorts: 2–3 days. Long-form YouTube videos: 5–7 days. Rush delivery is available — just ask. I always communicate clearly on timelines before starting.

How do I get started? +

Drop me an email at raghavrathore04.rr76@gmail.com or DM me on Instagram @Raghav_2754. I'll get back to you within 24 hours to discuss your project and get things rolling.
